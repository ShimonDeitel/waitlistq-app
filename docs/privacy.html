Privacy Policy

Waitlist - Booking Queue is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private application-support storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or identifiers. The app makes no network requests and has no server backend.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit; we never see your payment information.

If a future version adds optional features requiring network access (such as iCloud sync), this policy will be updated first and you will be asked for consent.
